ABOUT US

South West Planning Consultancy

South West Planning Consultancy is an independent practice based in the West Country, providing planning and design consultancy services throughout the area. We work with a range of long-standing clients providing a ‘one-stop-shop’ to advise, protect and enhance the value and performance of land and property assets. 

We focus on providing our clients with tailored, deliverable solutions, assisting them to navigate the planning process. We deal with projects in the residential, commercial, agricultural and leisure sectors in the West Country serving private clients and landowners, developers, asset managers and their advisors.

We can assist with:

  • Planning Applications
  • Planning Appraisals
  • Planning Appeals
  • Resolution/Variation of Planning Conditions
  • Retrospective Planning
  • Lawfulness
  • Permitted Development
  • Enforcement
  • Architecture
  • Implementation
  • Housing & Strategic Development
  • Commercial & Mixed-Use Development
  • Waste Planning
  • Planning for Demolition
  • Equestrian Development
  • Leisure/Tourism Development
  • Renewables
Please contact us to discuss your requirements.
